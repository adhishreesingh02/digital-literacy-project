# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

As a student, I use the internet daily for studies, payments, and social media, so I feel it is very important to be aware of online safety. Based on what I learned about cybercrimes like UPI fraud, here are some simple precautions that I think every student should follow.

---

## General Safety Tips

- I try to use strong and different passwords for my accounts so that they are not easy to guess.  
- I never share my OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they sound genuine.  
- I avoid clicking on unknown links that I receive through messages or emails.  
- I make sure my phone and apps are updated regularly for better security.  
- I am careful while using public Wi-Fi, especially when logging into important accounts.  
- I always think before posting or sharing anything online.  

---

## UPI & Financial Safety Tips

- I never share my UPI PIN with anyone, no matter what they say.  
- Before approving any request, I always check whether I am sending or receiving money.  
- I avoid scanning QR codes or accepting payment requests from unknown people.  
- If I get any suspicious call related to bank or payments, I try to verify it from official sources.  

---

## Reporting Cybercrime

If I or someone I know faces any cybercrime, it is important to report it immediately:

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930  

---

## Conclusion

In my opinion, staying alert and being careful online can prevent most cybercrimes. Following these simple steps can help us stay safe and protect our personal information.
